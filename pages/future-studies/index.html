Future Studies

Lejiaohao Future Studies (乐郊号未来学研究) contains essays and surveys on long-term futures under known physical constraints.

Scope

This area is for future-studies writing, astroengineering analysis, civilization-scale reasoning, and public explanatory essays.

  • Civilization-level evolution.
  • Astroengineering and megastructures.
  • Stellar engineering.
  • Post-biological civilization.
  • Long-term computation.
  • Energy and material flows.
  • Governance under advanced technological capacity.
