Lejiaohao Project

Lejiaohao Project (乐郊号项目) is an ongoing research-oriented project on future studies, astroengineering, megastructures, and civilization-scale design under established physical and engineering constraints.

Explore the Project GitHub

Overview

Future reasoning under physical constraints

The project starts from established physics, observational constraints, and mainstream theoretical parameter ranges. It treats causality, unitarity, thermodynamics, finite resources, engineering limits, and sociological constraints as fundamental constraints on reasoning about physically possible futures.

EMITS

Five fundamental survival elements for macroscopic analysis of megastructure engineering and technological civilizations:

  • Energy
  • Information
  • Material
  • Transport
  • Stability
